About
London Sawada

I’m London. I’m in school for nursing, I served in the Navy, and I’m still building my art brain in public. This whole thing started because I did not know how to flirt, so I learned how to draw instead. I was born in Pocono Mountain, Pennsylvania, grew up between there and Japan, and now I’m in North Carolina. Cosminuity is an old alias that stuck, so it became the label for my art and animation. I’m taking my time and letting the story show up when it is ready.

How this started

This started the way most bad ideas start. I drew something once, got a little encouragement, and kept going. There was no plan, and there still is not one. Cosminuity became the place where all the unfinished ideas live.

What I care about

Personality over perfection. If something feels alive, a little weird, or slightly uncomfortable in a good way, I’m interested. Clean art is nice. Memorable art is better.

What I’m working on

I’m building a story without forcing it. Characters come first, then settings, then maybe a plot if I get lucky. Most days I’m just figuring out who these people are and why they exist.

I treat progress like a weekly habit, not a single big moment.

Why animation

Animation keeps me honest. If a design only works in one still frame, it usually falls apart the second it moves. Motion exposes weak ideas fast, which is painful but useful.

Where this is going

No roadmap. I’m aiming for steady progress, better decisions, and fewer abandoned folders. If it grows into something bigger, cool. If not, I still win because I’m improving.
